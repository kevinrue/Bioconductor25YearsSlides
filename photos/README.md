# Photo Contributions

This folder contains photos displayed in the
[Bioconductor 25 Years photo carousel](https://kevinrue.github.io/Bioconductor25YearsSlides/).

## How to contribute

1. **Fork** this repository.
2. **Add** your photo(s) to this `photos/` folder.
3. **Open a pull request** — automated checks will validate your photo(s).
4. Once merged, the carousel will update automatically.

## Naming convention

Photos are displayed in **alphabetical order** of their filename.
Use a naming scheme such as `YYYY-MM-DD-description.jpg` to control
where your photo appears in the slideshow.

Example: `2025-08-06-bioc-conference-keynote.jpg`

## File requirements

Automated checks will **reject** photos that exceed either of these limits:

| Limit | Value |
|-------|-------|
| File size | 5 MB |
| Maximum dimension (width or height) | 3 000 px |

**Supported formats:** `.jpg` / `.jpeg`, `.png`, `.gif`, `.webp`

> **Tip:** Use a tool such as [Squoosh](https://squoosh.app/) to resize or
> compress your photo before submitting.
